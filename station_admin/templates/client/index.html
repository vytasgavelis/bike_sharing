Parking

In order to use parking services you must visit any site that provides parking services. You can view available sites in the parking map.

After you arrive to the site scan the QR code on the door and you will be taken to that sites window. Then you can select to either park your bike or scooter and begin your parking session.

Finally when you want to take back your vehicle, visit the site and scan the QR code again, press "End parking" button and that's it!

Renting

To use renting services you must visit any site that provide renting services. You can view these sites in the rent map.

After you arrive to the site scan the QR code of the vehicle and you will be taken to that vehicles window. Then you can press "Start rent" button and your vehicle will be unlocked.

Finally when you want to return the vehicle you can do that any site that has unoccupied rent spots. You can view them in the rent map. After you arrive to the site scan the QR code of the rent spot, park your vehicle into that rent spot and press the button "End rent". The vehicle will be locked and your rent session will be ended

Reservations

If you want to reserve vehicle in advance go to rent map, select site and you will see all available vehicles for rent. Then click "Reserve" button and the vehicle will be reserved. Keep in mind you can only have up to one reservation maximum.
